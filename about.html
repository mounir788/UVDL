ABOUT US

The Drink Locker, a groundbreaking initiative has redefined the standards for personal hygiene in public spaces. Recognizing the challenges posed by bacterial contaminants in public restrooms, our team has developed a state-of-the-art UV sanitizing unit. This unit is meticulously designed to safeguard beverages and personal items from the multitude of germs commonly found in these environments. With its sleek design, the device can be seamlessly integrated into various settings, providing users with a hands-free sanitization experience at the wave of a hand. Beyond its primary function, The Drink Locker stands as a testament to the future of public health solutions, aiming to elevate awareness and practices concerning cleanliness and health in communal areas.

The UV Drink Locker is a self-sanitizing unit designed for installation in public restrooms. It is a seamless integration that utilizes UV technology to kill a vast majority of germs and bacteria, including E. coli, salmonella, MRSA, and many others that are common in such settings. The activation is hands-free, initiated by a simple hand wave, ensuring minimal contact and maximum hygiene. The product represents not just a tool for cleanliness but a stride towards health-conscious living in public spaces.

Our product is designed with utmost safety in mind, ensuring that
beverages remain untouched and secure. The Drink Locker provides an
added layer of protection against unauthorized access, safeguarding
your drink from potential tampering while prioritizing discretion and
mindfulness.
